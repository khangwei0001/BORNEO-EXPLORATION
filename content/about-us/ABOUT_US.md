# ABOUT US

- First established in 1991, Borneo Exploration Tours & Travel Sdn Bhd has operated more than two decades starting as a travel agent and consultant around Borneo. After a decade of our business, we have expanded our services throughout the world specializing in all kinds of Tourism Services from Local and Adventure Tours, Air-Ticketing, Hotel Booking, Cruise Holidays and many more. We are also known as the Cruise Specialist for cruise vacation.

- We are the ground handling agent of MAS “Golden Holidays” package (Let’s Do it In Sarawak) as well as Air Asia’s “Jom Sarawak” and Transmile Air “Budget Holidays” package. We are also the officially appointed ground handler for the International Arrivals of several major wholesalers based in Kuala Lumpur and Singapore.

- In the year 2001, we are recognized as one of the Top 3 Tour Operator in Sarawak for the “Hornbill Tourism Award 2000”. We’ve also won awards from Genting-Resort World Berhad for “Best Supporting East Malaysia Agent 2001" and ”Top Achievers for East Malaysia Operator 2002". Additionally, we’ve also won the “Sarawak Hornbill Tourism Award 2009/2010” and “Sarawak Hornbill Tourism Award 2015/2016”.

- We are licensed by the Ministry of Tourism (MOTOUR) now known as Ministry of Tourism and Culture (MOTAC) under KKKP No. 2213 for Inbound Travel, Outbound Travel and Ticketing. We are approved members of International Air Transport Association (IATA) and the Malaysian Association of Tour & Travel Agents (MATTA). We are also members of Sarawak Tourist Federation (STF) and Association of Sarawak Inbound Agencies (ASIA).
