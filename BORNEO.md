# 1. What this website is / what it does

Borneo Explorer is a **Malaysian travel agency and tour operator** that offers both domestic and international travel services. While the company originally focused on tours within Borneo, it has expanded into a full-service travel agency offering a wide range of holiday and travel solutions.

## Main Services

- Borneo and local Malaysian tour packages
- International holiday packages
- Adventure and eco-tourism experiences
- Flight booking and ticketing
- Hotel reservations
- Cruise holiday packages
- Travel consultation and itinerary planning
- Group, corporate, incentive, and educational tours
- Pilgrimage and special-interest tours

The primary goal of the website is to showcase travel destinations and encourage visitors to enquire about or book travel packages.

# 2. General Theme, Style, and Design

## Overall Theme

The website has a **travel brochure** aesthetic focused on inspiring visitors to travel. It emphasizes exploration, holidays, adventure, and relaxation through large destination photography.

The overall feeling is:

- Tropical
- Vacation-oriented
- Adventure-focused
- Professional travel agency
- Family-friendly
- Aspirational and inspirational
